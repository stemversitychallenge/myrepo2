About

What is our mission?

College education is important, and so is paying for tuition! In our country, thousands of students interested in STEM are unable to pursue higher education due to their financial standing. Particularly, women and underrepresented minorities represent a small fraction of college bound STEM students.

This is why the blank scholarship was established. By providing an opportunity for women and underrepresented minorities to win a college scholarship, we hope to insight greater STEM interest within these groups.

Blank scholarship is provided by MyDeltaSchool, an online education provider established in 2012. MyDeltaSchool has been providing quality education in a variety of subjects related to STEM.
